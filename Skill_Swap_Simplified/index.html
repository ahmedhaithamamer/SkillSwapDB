How SkillSwap Works

SkillSwap is a platform that helps university students exchange skills and knowledge without money. Here's how to get started and make the most of our community.

1. Create Your Profile

Sign up with your university email and list the skills you can teach others. Be specific about what you offer, your expertise level, and time commitment.

2. Browse Skills

Explore the marketplace to find skills offered by other students. Filter by category, skill level, or use the search to find exactly what you need.

3. Connect & Message

When you find someone with a skill you want to learn, or when someone requests a skill you can teach, send them a message to start the conversation.

The SkillSwap Code

Respect Time

Show up on time for your sessions and respect your partner's schedule.

Be Patient

Everyone learns at different speeds. Be patient when teaching others.

Give Constructive Feedback

Share constructive feedback to help your partner improve.

Pay it Forward

Help others with skills you've learned to keep our community thriving.
